Full-Spectrum Support

What IAI Brings
to Every Case

Most attorneys underutilize investigators. Here is the complete scope of what IAI delivers — from the moment you receive the case file to the day your client walks out of court.

Service 01
Case File Review & Agency Policy Analysis
We read the entire file — police reports, body cam logs, chain of custody records — looking for inconsistencies, policy violations, and constitutional issues. Every law enforcement agency has written SOPs. We compare what officers did against what their own policies required and document every deviation.
Service 02
Pre-Sentence Investigation (PSI) Review
Independent line-by-line analysis of the probation officer’s PSI report. We identify misclassified prior convictions, inflated criminal history scores, unsupported guideline calculations, and overlooked mitigating factors. We deliver a written analysis you can use directly in sentencing objections and oral argument.
Service 03
Witness Location & Interview
We locate and interview witnesses law enforcement may have missed, ignored, or discouraged — building the alternative narrative your client deserves. All statements are documented and preserved in court-ready format. Subject locate services available across Harris, Fort Bend, and surrounding counties.
Service 04
EyeDetect® Credibility Assessment
Two Converus-certified EyeDetect® Examiners on staff. We evaluate the credibility of your client, witnesses, and informants using scientifically validated ocular-motor technology — with up to 95% accuracy, no physical contact, and results in under 30 minutes. PCSOT-compliant testing also available.
Service 05
Mitigation Investigation
Comprehensive background investigations uncovering mental health history, trauma, family dynamics, and character witnesses. We build the full picture that courts need to sentence fairly — the human context that no probation report adequately captures.
Service 06
Expert Testimony & Consultation
Dr. Walwyn provides expert testimony on law enforcement procedure, use of force, agency policy compliance, and investigative conduct — with credentials no opposing expert can match. Available for affidavits, deposition preparation, and court testimony in federal and state proceedings.

Credibility Technology

EyeDetect® Credibility Assessment

EyeDetect® is a patented credibility assessment technology by Converus that measures involuntary eye behavior — pupil dilation, blink rate, and reading patterns — while a subject answers a series of questions. Unlike polygraph, it is non-invasive, requires no physical attachment, and produces a digitally scored result.

IAI has two Converus-certified EyeDetect® Examiners on staff — one of only a handful of firms in the Houston area with this capability. We administer examinations for defense preparation, client screening, informant challenge, and PCSOT court-required testing.
